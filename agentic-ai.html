What is Agentic AI?

Agentic AI represents the next evolution in artificial intelligence - autonomous agents that can perceive their environment, make decisions, and take actions to achieve specific goals without constant human supervision. These intelligent systems learn from experience, adapt to changing conditions, and continuously optimize their performance.
